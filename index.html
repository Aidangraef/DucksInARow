Guess the Right Order!

Ducks of Legend! The Conducktor has ordered these ducks in a row, and it is up to you to discover the correct order!

Play cards to find where these Ducks are, and if your guess is closest to the correct order, you win!

But be careful not to draw the Goose card! Or the round might be over quicker than you think!

About us

Graph Games was started by Aidan Graef in Dayton Ohio. Aidan needed a studio name to release his first ever board game under. Ducks in a Row aims to be the first in a long list of successful launches in Graph Games' inventory. Their mission is to create games that help bring fun and enjoyable experiences for players to share in with their friends.
